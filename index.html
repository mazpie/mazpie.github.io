Pietro Mazzaglia

I am a PhD researcher in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Ghent. The objective of my research is to build intelligent agents that discover and learn how to behave in the environment by interacting with it.

PhD Research

The main focus of the research is on unsupervised objectives for model-based control. Some topics I am interested in are: reinforcement learning, exploration, world models, representation learning, and active inference.
